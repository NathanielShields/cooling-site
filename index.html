The Scale of the Crisis

Heat Is Already a Catastrophe

Across the United States, outdoor construction, agriculture, logistics, and utility crews are already losing work time to dangerous wet-bulb temperatures. Similar conditions now routinely affect workers in South Asia, sub-Saharan Africa, and Southeast Asia — the physiological limits of outdoor labor are being tested today.

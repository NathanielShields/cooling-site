Labor Productivity vs. Temperature

Acclimatized workers' sustainable work capacity drops sharply above 28°C. At 32°C, physically demanding tasks (400–500 W, e.g. heavy lifting or operating manual tools for extended periods) fall below 20% capacity.

Figure: Smallcombe et al., Int J Biometeorol 66, 2463–2476 (2022)

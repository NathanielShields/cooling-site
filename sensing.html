How It Works

Three Layers of Intelligence

1

Real-Time Biometric Monitoring

A chest-mounted sensor continuously measures core body temperature (±0.2°C), heart rate (±2 bpm), skin temperature, and derived physiological Heat Strain Index (HSI). Comparable accuracy to CORE 2 at half the price.

±0.2°CCore temp accuracy
±2 bpmHeart rate accuracy
5–7 daysBattery per charge
2

Individualized Break Scheduling

Rather than applying generic blanket schedules, the platform drives per-worker work/rest cycles from each person's real-time physiological state. This means breaks are taken when each individual actually needs them, not on a fixed timer, extending productive work time while maintaining safety. Notley et al. (2023) show individualized schedules can extend work cycles without jeopardizing safety. Wearable sensor fatigue prediction achieves ≥85% accuracy (Umer et al., 2020).

3

Predictive Weather-Integrated Scheduling

Per-worker heat response models trained on individual biometric history generate productivity forecasts for upcoming weather up to 7 days out, enabling proactive shift scheduling, crew rotation, and pre-cooling recommendations before conditions become critical.
